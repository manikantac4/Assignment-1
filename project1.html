Grand Canyon, USA

The Grand Canyon is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in Arizona, United States. It is one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World.

Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is an ancient Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains in Peru. It is renowned for its sophisticated dry-stone walls that fuse huge blocks without the use of mortar.

Victoria Falls, Zambia/Zimbabwe

Victoria Falls is a waterfall on the Zambezi River at the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe. It is one of the largest and most famous waterfalls in the world, known for its stunning beauty and powerful flow.

Great Barrier Reef, Australia

The Great Barrier Reef is the world's largest coral reef system, located off the coast of Queensland, Australia. It is home to an incredible diversity of marine life and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Aurora Borealis, Various Locations

The Aurora Borealis, also known as the Northern Lights, is a natural light display that occurs in the polar regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It is caused by the interaction between charged particles from the sun and the Earth's atmosphere.
